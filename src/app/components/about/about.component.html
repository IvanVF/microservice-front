Company history

VeloFreedom.com was founded in Moscow in 2004. During our work, more than 60,000 people have become our satisfied customers. Today "VeloFreedom.com" is a company with an established team of professionals with significant practical experience and developing in the following areas: sale of bicycles, accessories, spare parts, as well as maintenance and repair.

The purpose and objectives of the company

We achieve the most important goal of our business policy, which is to fully satisfy the interests and needs of customers. VeloFreedom.com has established itself as a reliable and strong partner with stable partnerships with the world's leading brands.

During its work on the market, "VeloFreedom.com" has become one of the most famous bicycle shops in the World! TheCompany's plans for the future include expanding the range of products and the list of services provided, improving the quality of customer service and maintaining leadership in the Global World bicycle market.

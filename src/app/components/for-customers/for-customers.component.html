Return and exchange

We return and exchange goods in accordance with consumer protection law. In disputable situations, we always try to meet the needs of the customer!

Address and return date

Return and exchange of goods is carried out at the address where the goods were purchased. If the product was purchased via delivery, then the return is carried out in our retail store.

The maximum period during which the goods can be returned to the seller is 14 days from the date of purchase

Return and exchange is carried out on weekdays from 12.00 to 20.00.

Return conditions

Return of goods of good quality is possible if:

  • no more than 14 days have passed since the purchase,
  • there is a cash and sales receipt,
  • The item is not included in the list of non-refundable items.

Cars, bicycles, scooters, motorcycles are not subject to return and exchange

Return of goods of inadequate quality (malfunction or failure to fulfill their functional qualities) is possible if:

  • a cash and sales receipt saved,
  • there are no signs of improper operation or signs of mechanical impacts (bumps, dents, traces after falls), which could result in a malfunction.

Return procedure

When returning goods of good quality, the full cost of the goods is returned to the buyer, without the cost of delivery.

When returning goods of inadequate quality, the buyer is refunded the full cost of the goods and the cost of delivery.

Cost of the goods is paid to the buyer in cash.
